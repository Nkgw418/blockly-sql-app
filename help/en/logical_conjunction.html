AND

The block

The and-block symbolises the 'AND'-statement in SQL. The 'AND' operator displays a record if both the first condition 'AND' the second condition are true.
It is used directly in the 'WHERE'-section.
It can be extended by clicking on the star-icon

Functionality

When you click on the star-icon, a new workspace appears.

In this workspace you can drop blocks into an 'ADD'-block to expand the 'AND'-block. So for each droped 'AND'-block one new 'AND' appears.Like you see in the picture below.
If you want to reduce the number of and's, you click on the star icon and pull the 'AND'-blocks out of the 'ADD'block.
