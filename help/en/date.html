Date variable

The block

This block symbolises date variables. It is used as a value in the compare operator, in the "IF" and "IF NULL" of the otherfunction, in datefunctions.

Functionality

The default value of this variable is the current date.

This value can be changed, as you see in the picture to the left.
First click on the date. While you click on the date a calendar appears. In this calendar you can choose the year via dropdown and the month via the arrow-buttons.
After choosing year and month klick on the date of the day.
After choosing the day, the calender disapears and the date is set.
The calendar has two additional buttons. The 'this month'-button to return to the current month an the 'close'-button, which closesthe calendar.
