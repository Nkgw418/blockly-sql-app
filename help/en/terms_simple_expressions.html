Mathematically operator

The block

This block symbolises the simple mathematic operations '+','-','+' and '/'. For all other mathematic operations use the numberfunctions . It is used with the compare operator.

Functionality

The Block has one dropdown, with the operations. So just click in the dropdown and choose your operation.
The values, which you can plugg in in this block, are the block itself, numeric values and columns and numberfunctions. Any other block will be unplugged.
